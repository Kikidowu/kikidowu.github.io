Noodles with a Twist

This is a recipe for turning your basic noodles into a healthy colorful meal.

Why Noodles with a Twist

Noodles with a twist as I like to call it is quick and easy to make. It is my go-to meal for lunch on days when I have back to back meetings and don't have time to cook a more elaborate meal.

What you need

  • 2 packs of uncooked noodles (any brand of your choice)
  • 1 cup of Frozen bellpeppers
    • Red bellpepper
    • Orange bellpepper
    • Yellow bellpepper
  • 1 cup of Mixed vegetables
  • A quarter of 1 Habanero (optional)
  • Half clove of Garlic
  • 1 medium sized Onion
  • Protein of your choice. (I like Shrimp so Shrimp it is for me)
  • 4 tablespoons of Vegetable oil

How to make it! Let's Get into It!!!

Step 1- Meal Prep

  • Shop for everything on the "what you need" list
  • Wash up all your vegetables with Vinegar
  • Chop all the vegetables that need to be chopped
    • Onions, Bellpeppers, Gralic, Habanero, etc
  • Have your choice of protein cleaned and ready to go.

Step 2- Cook

Please note that this step requires multi-tasking so please be prepared to joggle multiple things at the same time.

Grab a small pot and a wok pan
Add 2 cups of water to the small pot and bring to a boil.
While water is boiling, add 2 table spoon of vegetable oil to your wok pan.
After about 1minute, add onions and protein of choice to the wok pan and let that cook for another 3-5minutes. (Please add salt and other seasonigs of your preference for taste)
While your protein is cooking, check on your small pot of boiling water and add the 2 packs of noodles for 2-3minutes.
Go back to your wok pan and scoop out the protein from the pan.
Then add 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil. After about a minute, add in all the veggies, bellpeppers, garlic, etc.
Let that cook for about 4 minutes (give this a good stir and season accordingly for taste)
In the meantime, go back to your pot of noodles and drain out the liquid. (turn of the burner and focus on the wok )
Add your cooked protein into the wok pan with the mixed vegetables
Then add your cooked noodles and give it all a good stir for another minute or two.
Taste for flavor.
And you are good to go.

Step 3- Enjoy your Noodles with a Twist

Plate your meal and enjoy!
You earned it!
