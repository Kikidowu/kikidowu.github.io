The Six C's of Inclusive Leadership

There is a thin line between good and great leaders. In many organizations today, individual contributors who are excelling in their roles are often promoted to people managers with little to no experience managing people. The fact that an individual is a top performer doesnt make them a great leader or People Manager. In this resource, you will be learning about the Six C's of Inclusive Leadersgip. Regardless of where you are in your Leadership journey, praciticing these values will help you stand out and become a leader that truly empowers

What are these Six Cs/Values

Please note that there are so many Leadership values that you can embody to be an exceptional leader. Values are often a reflection of the culture and context you find yourself in. Feel free to pick and choose anyone of these that align with your sphere of influence and/or organizational culture.

  • Curiousity
  • Courage
  • Cultural Intelligence
  • Cognizance
  • Collaboration
  • Commitment
